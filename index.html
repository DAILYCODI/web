01 Hexapod Design

A hexapod robot is a six-legged robotic system designed for versatile movement on various terrains. Each leg typically consists of multiple joints, allowing for agile and adaptive locomotion. With its unique structure, the robot can scan the terrain, and provide video feed while being able maneuver around.

02 Computer Vision

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence that enables machines to interpret and understand visual information from images or videos. Using algorithms and deep learning models, computer vision systems can identify objects, recognize patterns, and mimics human visual perception for tasks like image recognition, object detection, and facial recognition.

03 LiDAR

LiDAR, which stands for Light Detection and Ranging, is a remote sensing technology that uses laser pulses to measure distances to objects and create detailed 2D representations of the environment. By emitting light beams and measuring their reflections, LiDAR systems generate precise maps, capturing terrain, structures, and objects with high accuracy.
